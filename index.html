about me

Hey, I'm Nathan - a web and games developer from Perth, Australia.

I'm a real sucker for the intersection between design and code -
that sweet spot where pretty things turn into something interactive, engaging, and memorable. It's real fun!

Bold, experimental designs with striking colour schemes are my favourite -- I love to embrace the weird and wonderful.

My work with games has taught me plenty about optimising for the best user experience, and how to create snappy animated screens that don't take forever to load.

If that sounds like your kind of thing too, let's team up!
